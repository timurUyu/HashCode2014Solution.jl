HashCode2014Solution.ComputeUpperBound — Method
ComputeUpperBound(city, duration_limit)

Algorithm to compute upper bound on distance. Compares velocity of each street and constructs 8 separate paths for each car in a "drag race" style journey. This approach relaxes the constraint on connectivity of the streets.

Fields

  • 'city::City': a street in the city
  • 'duration_limit::Int64': the total time limit imposed on cars traversing streets
